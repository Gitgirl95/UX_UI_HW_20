Travel Wise is an app that does all the planning and booking for your trips!

Problem Statement

People who travel are tired of having to use multiple apps to book all their travel needs and staying on budget during the process.

Solution

PCreate one app that does it all–all the booking for flights, stays, transportation, and activities.

Research

Interviews Testing Empathy Mapping User Journey Maps Storyboarding.

Findings

Users are worried about the time it takes from them to plan and compare everything they have to book for their trips in multiple locations. They want to do everything from booking to budgeting in one app.
